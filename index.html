Privacy Policy for Capture List

Effective Date: June 27, 2025

Capture List (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application Capture List.

1. Information We Collect

Capture List does not collect or share personal data with the developer or any third parties. All information entered into the app is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData and SQLite technology.

We do not have access to your stored information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since your information is stored locally on your device, we do not access, collect, or use your personal information in any way.

The app is designed for content creators to organize and store things they want to capture for events. All information stays on your device unless you choose to share or back it up through your own means (e.g., iCloud, device backup).

3. Third-Party Services

Capture List does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking tools.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information stored within the app. However, please note that no method of storage is 100% secure.

We recommend keeping your device updated and protected to maintain the security of your data.

5. Children's Privacy

Capture List is suitable for users of all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. As no data is shared with us, there is no risk of children's personal information being transmitted to the developer.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Changes will be posted within the app if applicable.
